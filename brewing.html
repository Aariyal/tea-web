Brewing Guide

Five simple steps to make the perfect cup of tea.

How to Brew Tea

1

Boil Fresh Water

Always use fresh, cold water. Reboiled water has less oxygen and can make tea taste flat.

2

Warm Your Cup

Pour a little hot water into your cup to warm it, then pour it out. This keeps your tea hotter for longer.

3

Add Tea

Use one teaspoon of loose tea or one tea bag per cup. Place it in your cup or teapot.

4

Pour and Steep

Pour the hot water over the tea. Let it steep for 3–5 minutes depending on how strong you like it.

5

Enjoy

Remove the tea bag or strain the leaves. Add milk, honey or lemon if you wish. Sit back and enjoy!
